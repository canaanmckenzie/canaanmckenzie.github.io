about

I explore offensive security - vulnerabilities, exploits both for web and hardware. I enjoy the challenge of finding unique ways to improve this internet ecosystem by gently pulling apart the Gordian Knot. The tools of my trade are Rust, C/C++, Python, JS, whatever else, and every possible OSS tool where I can work and contribute. I'm currently available for [more] remote contracts/consulting and/or full time employment in the cybersecurity space, feel free to reach out!

If you would like to check out some of my stuff, see my weblog, follow my Twitter, and/or Github ALSO! if you would like buy me a cup of coffee Patreon :).
