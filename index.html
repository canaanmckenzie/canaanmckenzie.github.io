about

I'm Canaan McKenzie, I am a former scientist having worked in biotech, specifically developing processes to rapdily test and analyze novel vaccines using industrial fluid handling robots. I got that job after building by scratch a 3D printer for the development of replicable and replacible knee tissue in college - shambles. Currently I'm a cybersecurity researcher, hacker, and web developer. I have a decade of programming experience (Python, JS, Go, Ruby - Ruby on Rails, C++, React, Lisp, and Linux system administration). I'm interested in developing funtional, secure web applications with a focus on scalability and reliability. I'm also currently developing a roguelike game in Rust for my own education and interests. I'm available for remote contract and/or full time employment, feel free to reach out!

If you would like to check out some of my stuff, see my weblog, follow my Twitter, and/or Github ALSO! if you would like buy me a cup of coffee Patreon :).
