Westworld Sublime Theme

Making a custom Sublime Text 3 Theme

I had been wanting to do this for a while. I often find myself moving between pre-built Sublime themes every few months, never fully satisfied with the colors or aesthetic. I thought it would be nice to write a quick guide for making a custom one if anyone is interested in doing the same. I've used a fair few IDEs (although I'm not sure Sublime truly falls into this category), but for my current aesthetic and workflow it's been nice and simple.

As much as Lisp and Vim are familiar to me, I like the bare bones of working in a nice simple text editor. Maybe I'll change in the future, but I've been programming professionally since 2014 and it's held up all this time.

As of yesterday (10/6/2022) I heard that Westworld had been cancelled off its home on HBO. I found the first season of westworld a breath of fresh air, mostly because that's when I was building a 3D printer for tissue engineering while doing research at the University of Vermont. It hit quite close to home. So I thought I would take some time and build the closest approximation to the IDE shown briefly in the show.
